## 3. Consolidated Boss Data (For Article Writing)

### Basic Info
- **Full Name:** Heolstor, the Nightlord (Night Aspect)
- **Role:** Final Boss / Last Nightlord
- **Location:** Night Aspect expedition, Day 3
- **Lore:** Name from Old English meaning "darkness/obscurity". The origin of the Night itself.

### How to Unlock
Defeat 4 Nightlords first:
1. Nightreign Tricephalos (first Nightlord)
2. Maris, Fathom of Night (Augur)
3. Gnoster, Wisdom of Night (Sentient Pest)
4. Fissure in the Fog (Caligo)

### HP Values (by player count: solo / duo / trio)
- Phase 1 (Shape of Night): 4,984 / 9,968 / 14,952
- Phase 2 (True Form): 11,214 / 22,428 / 33,642

### Weaknesses & Immunities
| Category | Phase 1 | Phase 2 |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Holy | +35% | +20% |
| Fire | +20% | +20% |
| Lightning | — | +20% |
| Pierce | +10% | +15% |
| Slash | +15% | -10% |
| Strike | -10% | +10% |
| **Immune** | Poison, Bleed, Frostbite | Poison, Bleed, Frostbite |
| **Applicable** | Scarlet Rot, Sleep | Scarlet Rot, Sleep, Madness |

### Recommended Level
- Minimum: Level 13
- Recommended: Level 14

### Phase Breakdown

**Phase 1: Shape of Night (Sword)**
- Slow, spectral knight with single greatsword
- Guard Counters effective, backward dodging reliable
- Key: CONSERVE healing flasks and Ultimates

**Phase 2: Shape of Night (Greatsword) — at ~60% HP**
- Draws second sword, becomes aggressive
- Multi-dash high-speed slashes
- Thrust attacks with minimal windup
- Low poise — can be staggered with heavy attacks

**Phase 3: Heolstor Awakened (Moonlight Greatsword) — 2nd health bar starts**
- Magic + dark elemental attacks
- Chain combos ending with AoE
- Ground drag → multiple projectiles (dodge sideways)
- Darkness channeling with homing projectiles (can interrupt with Ultimate)

**Phase 4: Heolstor (Ground Eruption) — at ~60% of 2nd bar**
- Flying slam → delayed ground explosions + fissures
- Random elemental infusion from other Nightlords
- New crouching combo requiring dash-through
- Final slam = extreme damage (need Greatshield / i-frames / Ultimate)

### Named Attacks
- Backstab (teleport + 3-hit combo)
- Dashing Slash (linear dash + sword strike)
- Pouncing Combo (3 side-to-side slashes)
- Winding Thrust (windup + ground impale)
- Cover of Darkness (channel + random projectiles)
- Night of the Lord (fly + slam + explosive fissures)
- Flaming Fissures (slash series + ground explosions)
- Sky Splitter (massive directional blast, frost/madness)

### Best Team Compositions
| Role | Best Pick | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Tank | Guardian | Greatshield + revival Ultimate + aggro control |
| DPS (Ranged) | Ironeye | Consistent ranged damage, safe revives, Eagle Eye for drops |
| DPS (Melee) | Wylder | Grappling hook gap-closer, Flaming Stake staggers boss |
| Alt DPS | Duchess | Confluence passive chains Ultimates, Loathsome Hex AoE |
| Alt DPS | Raider | Retaliate Skill boosts team damage Phase 2 |
| Solo Pick | Executor | Superior deflection, best solo survivability |

**Meta Comps:**
- Safe: Guardian + Ironeye + Wylder
- Nuclear DPS: Wylder + Ironeye + Duchess
- Solo: Executor (buy Wending Grace before Day 3)

### Pre-Fight Checklist
- [ ] Reach Level 13+ (14 recommended)
- [ ] Equip Holy damage weapon
- [ ] Acquire Scarlet Rot weapon/incantation as secondary
- [ ] Collect max healing flask charges (visit gold Churches)
- [ ] Buy Wending Grace (solo only)
- [ ] Equip elemental damage negation buffs
- [ ] Slot Holy-boosting Relics

### Rewards
- **Night of the Lord** — Unique relic (blue vessel slot)
- **Primordial Nightlord's Rune** — Currency
- **Sacred Erdtree Grail** — Unlocks 3 Yellow Relic slots for all characters
- **Nightfarer Skins** — New cosmetics available at Dresser
- **Remembrance Cutscene** — Character-specific ending (if Remembrance completed)
